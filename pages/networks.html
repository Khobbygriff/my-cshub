Understanding Networks

Computer networks are a set of computers connected together for sharing resources. They enable communication and data sharing between devices.

Types of Networks

- PAN (Personal Area Network): A network that connects devices in a very limited area.
- LAN (Local Area Network): A network that connects computers in a limited area.
- WAN (Wide Area Network): A network that covers a broad area, often connecting multiple LANs.
- MAN (Metropolitan Area Network): A network that covers a city or a large campus.
- VPN (Virtual Private Network): A secure network that uses encryption to protect data.
